Simply put, this is a private/public open-source fork of 2009Scape that <b>DOES NOT ACCEPT DONATIONS</b>.

### What is Taco Shack

Taco Shack is an unofficial remake of the 2009 era of RS that is usually forgotten.
Made by the 2009Scape team, edited and revised by John Money and Chat GPT.

### History of Taco Shack

The Taco Shack you see today is a fork of 2009Scape, it stays true to their main goal, with QoL improvements and a small amount of custom content. This all started as a "private" server that I made for my discord server. It will be nothing more than that. The website, discord and game are public but most of it's players came directly from my friend group.
